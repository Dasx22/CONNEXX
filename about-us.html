Who are we?
We at CONNEXX always cherish the nature and help connecting you the nature. Our website features a wide and attractive range of wildlife tours, photo trips, bird watching adventures.Each Connexx tour is well organised, competitively priced, environmentally responsible and fully customisable. Tours are led by highly qualified and experienced tour leaders. We also offer tailor-made packages for smaller groups, individuals and corporates.

Connexx is backed by highly successful entrepreneurs and run by a professional team, who are passionate about wildlife and have been involved in nature conservation activities over the years. We have experienced travel industry professionals and wildlife experts working for us who are instrumental in creating the best tour programs for our customers. Travel with us and redefine your holiday experience. Marvel at nature and admire the magnificent wildlife with which we share our planet.
Our Mission
Trees provide so many benefits to our everyday lives. They filter clean air, provide fresh
drinking water, help curb climate change, and create homes for thousands of species of
plants and animals. Planting a billion trees can help save the Earth from climate change
and biodiversity loss. When we restore and conserve critical forests, we remove carbon
and support biodiversity. A billion is a big number, but we know we can do it together.

So, we plant a tree for each transaction you make, thus, gradually approaching our goal.
